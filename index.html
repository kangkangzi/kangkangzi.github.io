Research Interests

Graph Prompt Learning: LLMs is currently a hot field attracting scientists worldwide. Training and fine-tuning LLMs to solve downstream tasks has shown outstanding performance in many domains, such as Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision. It is natural to consider whether we can use LLMs to solve tasks in the graph domain. However, due to their non-linear structure, graphs are harder for LLMs to understand compared to texts and images. Proposing a paradigm that leverages the power of LLMs to enhance GNNs' performance is a significant challenge.

I am currently interested in this field and conducting research under the supervision of Prof. Zhen Wang at Sun Yat-Sen University. I have also set up a site to collect some of the papers I have read related to this domain. Here is the site link: Graph Prompt Learning Papers.
